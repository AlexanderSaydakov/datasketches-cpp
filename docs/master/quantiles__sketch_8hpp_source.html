517 /* Serialized sketch layout:
518 * Long || Start Byte Addr:
519 * Addr:
520 * || 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7 |
521 * 0 || Preamble_Longs | SerVer | FamID | Flags |----- K ---------|---- unused -----|
522 *
523 * || 8 | 9 | 10 | 11 | 12 | 13 | 14 | 15 |
524 * 1 ||---------------------------Items Seen Count (N)--------------------------------|
525 *
526 * Long 3 is the start of data, beginning with serialized min and max item, followed by
527 * the sketch data buffers.
528 */
